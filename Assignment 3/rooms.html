Room One

Our two-bedroom, one-bathroom, fully self-contained rooms offer contemporary design with Smart TV. A fully equipped kitchen, dining, private balcony and BBQ overlooking our manicured grounds. Wake up relaxed and refreshed after a restful nights sleep in the comfort of either the queen bedroom or second bedroom with two single beds, ideal for small families or couples. With stunning views of acreage gardens, diverse native wildlife and views over Bancroft Bay, this delightful room creates a soothing ambience that is perfect for your next tranquil country getaway.

Inside our rooms you will find:

  • Split system heating and air conditioning
  • Open plan living
  • Open gas fire place
  • Coffee machine
  • High-speed WI-FI
  • Heated towel rails
  • Complimentary toiletries
  • High quaility European linen and towels

Pricing: $250 per night, minimum two night stay
