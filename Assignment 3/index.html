My Hotel is a luxury hotel with the comforts of home located in Metung, East Gippsland, Victoria. We offer guests accommodation needs with state-of-the-art facilities including heated swimming pool set at 28c for all-year-round use, full size artificial grass tennis court, sauna, private jetty to moor your boat during your stay, kids play equipment area and games room with video games, table tennis, pool table to name a few - the kids love it!

About Us

Established in 1990 by Sam and Julie Smith and officially opened in 1992 by iconic singing Kylie Minogue, My Hotel started with six rooms. The business was sold and expanded to include twenty units of accommodation. Each unit is decorated to reflex the seacoast environment we are located within and Hampton style theme.

With our attention to detail and emphasis on providing outstanding customer service, My Hotel was awarded four Victorian and two Australian Tourism Awards.
